ABOUT THE BAND

↕ drag photo

Toronto’s Premier Cover Band

Almost Famous GTA brings the energy and excitement of your favourite artists to every stage we touch. Spanning rock, dance, pop, and the greatest hits of the 80s, 90s, and 00s, our massive repertoire keeps audiences dancing from the first song to the last.

Whether it’s an intimate pub night or a large corporate event, Almost Famous GTA delivers professional, high-energy performances tailored to every crowd. We cover everyone from Depeche Mode and David Bowie to Bruno Mars and U2 — and much more.

Based in the Greater Toronto Area, we are available for bar gigs, private parties, corporate events, weddings, and festivals. Our setlist is fully customisable to suit your event and audience.
