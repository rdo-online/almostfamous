ABOUT THE BAND

↕ drag photo

Did you know that Mississauga is home to former NHL coach and Hockey Night in Canada commentator Don Cherry, KFC’s founder Colonel Sanders, and the world’s oldest mayor Hazel “Hurricane” McCallion?

You can add to that list now — the soon-to-be-famous rock cover band, Almost Famous GTA. You’re welcome, Mississauga.

Formed in early 2014, Almost Famous is a collection of guys and a gal who live and breathe live music. From David Bowie and The Clash to Bruno Mars and The Killers, from The Tragically Hip and U2 to Depeche Mode and Pat Benatar — if you know the words, we know the song.

Our mission is simple: bring the fun, energy, and excitement back into live music — whether that’s your favourite pub, a festival stage, a corporate event, or your wedding dance floor.

Spanning rock, dance, and pop across the 80s, 90s, 00s and beyond, our massive repertoire keeps audiences dancing from the first song to the last. Our setlist is fully customizable to suit your crowd.

We are Almost Famous: Rich, Richard, Donn, Paul, Jim and Vanessa.
